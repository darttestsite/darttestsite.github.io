Content

This website is going to be a portfolio, showcasing animation and creative technology projects that I developed over the past year.
The content will largely consist of images and videos of the artwork, along with a bit of text describing it.
One of the projects is related to climate change/flooding in Pakistan, and will be more activism and story-based than the others.

Design

The design of the website is going to be simple and clean. Since the content of the website will mostly be colorful images and videos of my artwork, I wanted to limit the amount of color in the design. This would also allow me to add more complicated JavaScript elements without making the website seem overwhelming for the user.
The text will be black with a white background, except for navigation links, which will have white text with a blue background. The headings will use an old-style Serif font, and the body will be a sans-serif font to provide contrast. There will be a 2px horizontal dividing line to separate different sections.

Interaction

The website will use CSS flexbox and grid. It is going to be interactive, making use of JavaScript libraries such as:
-Turn.Js to emulate a 3d book
-Curtains.Js to provide a ripple effect
-Locomotive Scroll for smooth scrolling and image transitions
The home-page will also feature an interactive visualizer that responds to the position of the cursor, made using Hydra.js
